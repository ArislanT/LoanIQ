# LoanIQ
Every year, students sign tens of thousands of dollars in student loans at 18 or 19 years old — often without understanding interest, repayment rules, or how policy changes affect their options. This isn’t about students borrowing too much: **it’s about borrowing badly**. Federal loans do accrue interest, costs are hidden, and missing key deadlines can add $2,000 to $10,000 in avoidable repayment costs—disproportionately harming financially illiterate, first-generation and immigrant families.


We want to build a financial decision tool that helps users understand both the meaning and consequences of their financial actions before they commit to them.The app uses familiar interface where it pulls verified loan options from official sources, explains each loan's implication in simple terms, displays upcoming state and federal law changes on student loans, and users are able to input loan and expense information and understand real-time tradeoffs such as total interest, payoff timelines, and missed payments,
